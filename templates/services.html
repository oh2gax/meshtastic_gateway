METAR, TAF & Solar Weather Service

Reply with weather or propagation data when a node sends a private message: wx efhk for the current METAR, taf efhk for the latest TAF forecast (any ICAO airport), or cmd solar for a compact solar/propagation summary (SFI, SN, X, A, K, AU, BZE, SW, Pf, Ef) from hamqsl.com. TAF and solar replies are truncated to fit one Meshtastic packet; the trailing characters that don't fit are dropped (an … is appended).
